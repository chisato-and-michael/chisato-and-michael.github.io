Where to stay

We recommend arriving in Whistler on Friday, September 18, 2026, as traffic on the Sea to Sky Highway can be unpredictable (we've been there stuck for hours…).

Group rates for the wedding are available at Nita Lake Lodge. To book, please call 1-888-755-6482 or email reservations@nitalakelodge.com, referencing "Chisato Ito & Michael Schneider Wedding" to access the group rate. Reservations must be made by Monday, July 20, 2026. A credit card is required at booking, with full payment due by the same date. Individual reservations may be cancelled without penalty up to 60 days prior to arrival.

There are also many accommodation options throughout Whistler Creekside and Whistler, including hotels, condos, and Airbnbs. We recommend booking soon, as weekends in Whistler fill up quickly.

Not sure where to stay? Reach out and we're happy to help :)
